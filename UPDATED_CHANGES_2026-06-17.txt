Updated workflow:
1. Admin creates/edits/deletes banks in POS Serial Management first.
2. Admin opens/selects a bank and adds POS serials under that bank.
3. Bank registration uses the admin-created bank dropdown.
4. Bank users do not select bank in Hardware. Their registered bank is auto-used.
5. Hardware POS serial dropdown is filtered by the registered/selected bank.
6. Admin Hardware still selects bank, then gets only that bank's POS serials.
7. Bank rename updates bankName references in users, tickets, field tasks, hardware batches and POS serials.
